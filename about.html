Judy Zheng

Thanks for visiting my gallery!

I am an artist currently located in Waterloo, ON. I have been taking art lessons since the age of three, under numerous art teachers as I moved from city to city. Over the years, I have progressed from pencil drawings to primarily oil painting, experimenting in many different mediums such as pen, watercolor, acrylic, clay, and mixed media.
